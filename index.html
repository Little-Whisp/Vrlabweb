Discover the experience

We have multiple experiences you can try out at VR Lab. We want students to have access to the most recent technology and introduce them to advanced tech! At VR lab you can try out games and other experiences. Beat Saber, Doom VFR, Bonelab and many more!

What tech do we have? Well a little bit of everything! VR, AR and MR. We have the Oculus Quest 2, HTC VIVE Pro 2 and the Hololense 2. You can try them all for free and students are also able to borrow our VR headsets. You can do that at the 'Stadslab balie'.

VR cheat sheet

It's important that you use VR in a safe and comfortable way. We have a couple tips in this cheat sheet that can help you with setting up the VR headset and create a safe and comfortable space for you and your friends!
